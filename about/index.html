About Me

Hi there! Here is Alex speaking.

Me?

I’m a self-taught software developer, rock climber/boulderer, and aviation enthusiast from HK.

Now living in Taipei for more than 9 years and graduated from NTNU Department of physics in 2020.
Currently, I’m working in (MSI)Micro-Star International Co. Ltd for over 2 years.

Why did I start programming?

The starting point of my programming career is that my job often needs to troubleshoot PC problems with Powershell.

It is easy to type a single commandlet on Powershell, but hard to write a script for the automation if I don’t have any programming logic. So I decided to learn the most friendly language which is Python.

Why am I blogging?

Currently, I’m working on an AI chatbot with Tensorflow and some front-end projects with JavaScript and Django. They are very exciting things, a lot of new knowledge needs to learn.

It is important to record the learning process systematically to digest and remember the new stuff.
That is the reason why I started this blog.
